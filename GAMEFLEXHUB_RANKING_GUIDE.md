## üéØ Phase 1: Immediate Indexing (Do This Now)

### 1. Verify Domain in Google Search Console

Since you have a custom domain (`gameflexhub.com`), DNS verification is best:

1. Go to **Google Search Console**
2. Click **Add Property**
3. Enter `gameflexhub.com` (use "Domain" option, not URL prefix)
4. Copy the **TXT record** provided
5. Go to your **Domain Provider** (Namecheap, GoDaddy, Vercel, etc.)
6. Add new TXT record:
   - Type: `TXT`
   - Name: `@` (or leave blank)
   - Value: `[Paste Google code]`
7. Wait 5 minutes, then click **Verify** in Search Console

### 2. Submit Sitemap

1. In Search Console
