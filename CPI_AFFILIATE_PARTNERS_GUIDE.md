## Tracking and Analytics

### What to Monitor:

1. **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**
   - Goal: 5-10% of visitors click your download links
   - Track which games get most clicks

2. **Conversion Rate**
   - Goal: 20-40% of clicks convert to installs
   - Low conversion? Change game offers

3. **Earnings Per Click (EPC)**
   - Goal: $0.10 - $0.50 per click
   - Higher EPC = better offer selection

4. **Best Performing Offers**
   - Focus on games that convert best
   - Drop low-performing offers

### Tools to Use:
- Google Analytics (track clicks)
- Each network's dashboard (track conversions)
- Pretty Links plugin (if using WordPress)
- Bitly (for link tracking)
