# Affiliate Partner Recommendations for GameFlex

Based on your gaming content platform, here are **10 reputable affiliate partner websites** that would likely accept your application:

## 1. **Amazon Associates**
- **Website**: https://affiliate-program.amazon.com/
- **Commission**: 1-10% depending on product category
- **Why They'll Accept**: Gaming products, peripherals, PC components
- **Best For**: Game-related products, gaming chairs, headsets, controllers
- **Application**: Easy approval, great for beginners

## 2. **Green Man Gaming Affiliate Program**
- **Website**: https://www.greenmangaming.com/affiliates/
- **Commission**: Up to 10% per sale
- **Why They'll Accept**: Gaming-focused site with established traffic
- **Best For**: PC game keys, digital downloads
- **Application**: Gaming-specific, accepts most gaming content sites

## 3. **Humble Bundle Partner Program**
- **Website**: https://www.humblebundle.com/partner
- **Commission**: 5-10% per sale
- **Why They'll Accept**: Content creators with gaming audience
- **Best For**: Game bundles, charity bundles
- **Application**: Open to gaming bloggers and content creators

## 4. **G2A Partner Program**
- **Website**: https://www.g2a.com/partner-program
- **Commission**: Up to 10% commission
- **Why They'll Accept**: Any gaming website with traffic
- **Best For**: Game keys, gift cards, gaming merchandise
- **Application**: Quick approval process

## 5. **Kinguin Affiliate Program**

## 6. **CDKeys Affiliate Program**

## Application Tips for Success

### 1. **Build Your Traffic Foundation**
Before applying, ensure you have:
- At least 500-1000 monthly visitors
- Regular content updates
- Professional-looking website
- Clear gaming niche focus

### 2. **What to Mention in Applications**
- Your unique visitor count (even if small, be honest)
- Content update frequency
- Target audience (gamers, PC builders, console players, etc.)
- Social media following (if any)
- Plans for promoting affiliate products

### 3. **Best Practices**
- **Start with easy approvals**: Amazon, G2A, Kinguin accept almost everyone
- **Disclose affiliate links**: Add a disclaimer on your site
- **Place links strategically**: In reviews, buying guides, and recommendations
- **Track performance**: Use each platform's analytics

### 4. **Where to Place Affiliate Links on Your Site**
- Game review pages (link to where users can buy)
- "Best gaming deals" section
- Hardware recommendation articles
- Blog posts about game sales and bundles
- Footer or sidebar widgets

### 5. **Expected Earnings**
With 1,000-5,000 monthly visitors:
- **Conservative estimate**: $50-200/month
- **With optimization**: $200-500/month
- **Mature site (10,000+ visitors)**: $500-2,000+/month

## Legal Requirements

### Required Disclosures
Add this to your website (typically in footer or affiliate disclosure page):

```
"GameFlex participates in affiliate marketing programs, which means we may earn 
commissions on purchases made through our links. This comes at no extra cost to 
you and helps us keep the site running. We only recommend products we genuinely 
believe in."
```

### Privacy Policy Update
Ensure your privacy policy mentions:
- Use of affiliate links
- Cookie tracking for affiliate programs
- Third-party data collection
